Why Choose Vijayasree Builders for Your Dream Home in Salem?

Vijayasree Builders stands as Salem's most trusted residential construction company, delivering exceptional quality homes since 1990. With over three decades of experience in the real estate industry, we have transformed the landscape of premium apartment living across Salem, Tamil Nadu.

Premium 2BHK and 3BHK Apartments in Salem's Prime Locations

Our portfolio includes thoughtfully designed 2BHK and 3BHK apartments ranging from 1100 to 1900 square feet, strategically located in Salem's most sought-after neighborhoods including Ammapet, Chinna Thirupathi, Suramangalam, Hasthampatti, Seelanaickenpatti, and Shevapet. Each project is meticulously planned to offer excellent connectivity to schools, hospitals, shopping centers, and major transportation hubs.

Quality Construction That Stands the Test of Time

At Vijayasree Builders, we never compromise on construction quality. Every project utilizes premium-grade materials, advanced construction techniques, and adheres to stringent quality control measures. Our buildings feature:

  • High-quality cement, steel, and aggregates from certified suppliers
  • Premium tiles, sanitaryware, and electrical fittings
  • Earthquake-resistant RCC framed structures
  • Superior waterproofing and weatherproofing solutions
  • Energy-efficient designs with optimal natural lighting and ventilation
  • Modern amenities including swimming pools, gymnasiums, and landscaped gardens

Transparent Dealings and Timely Delivery

Founded by Dr. Rajendran, Vijayasree Builders operates with complete transparency in all transactions. We maintain clear communication throughout the buying process, with no hidden costs or surprise charges. Our commitment to timely project delivery has earned us the trust of over 700 families who now call our projects home.
